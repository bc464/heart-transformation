"GIVING AND RECEIVING AN EVIL REPORT"

(Notes taken from Don Stephen's talk)

I am going to talk about what is an evil report and who are carriers of an evil report and what the effects of an evil report are. It is meat to my soul. Three months ago I went to a minister's seminar and two hours were spent on the subject - an evil report. It so answered some of the need's I've had in my life and put so many things together. I found I was a carrier of evil reports and a receiver of evil report and I didn't even know what they were, and I saw the hurt it was causing in the Body of Christ. It's a major cause of the hurts and wounds in the Body of Christ today.

Evil report involves a distortion of the facts or incomplete facts or even facts which you had no right to give - unauthorized facts; or facts given with the wrong motive. And when we do any of these things, they can cause people to come to an inaccurate conclusion and they'll respond in an unscriptural basis.

Evil report is so destructive it can separate long lasting friends. All of you have one or two close friends? You think how long it has taken you to develop that friendship. Proverbs 16:28 tells us that a whisper or gossip separate close friends. Now, you think of the hurts when a wisper, a bearer of evil report, separate a friendship you've worked on for so long.

There are several stages in which the body receives evil or sickness into it. We'll look at the physical stages and then some of the spiritual stages, which woud be paralled here.

IGNORANCE

The first stage in the body is what we'll call ignorance. You don't even know you are in the presence of carriers of a terrible germ. Now, for example, you can come to someone with a highly contagious disease but if you're notaware of it - that is the stage of ignorance; and you've not put this person into an isolation ward in the hospital - you've not isolated the germ. The minute you come into contact with them, that is the exposure stage. Your body is exposed to those infected and in fact you're in the first stage of infection and that infection leads to contamination. Your body becomes contaminated and you, yourself, become a carrier. So you are really infected, and if infected, you're a carrier of this disease. Lastly, this diseace begins to destroy the life support functions of your body.

So we'll look at the stages of ignorance, then, exposure to evil report; when you become a carrier of evil report; and the destructive effects evil report has on the Body of Christ.

2 Corinthians 2:11 "Don't be ignorant of the ways of the enemy or he will gain an advantage over us."

Ignorance is when we're not aware of the destructive power of distorted words. We're not truly aware of how hurtful words can be. If you give me a choice whether I can take a physical blow or a verbal blow from someone who can really hurt me with words, I'll take the physical blow any time.

Words can cause hurts so deep within us that we can spend hours, even years, trying to suppress the memory of those words. And that is why Proverbs says, "He who rules his own spirit is stronger than he who controls a city." If you rule and control your own spirit, you are not, in a moment of anger, going to lash out or hurt people with your words.

And we're got to come over this ignorant stage and understand how important it is how we speak and what we say. We are all very susceptible to what people think and what they say to us.

Each of us has a need to be loved and to be accepted. That is a God given need that is put in us. It can be met either through God Himself or through the world around us. That is why we have some of the things we do in the media today, where people are living for acceptance and world acclaim. It is a need within people which is being met by the world, other than the way God intended it to be met. Heavenly Father, I pray that each one of us might know how precious we are in your sight. The Tongue! Oh! That we can watch the tongue!

Men, when the Bible speaks to us particularly, in the New Testament and also in Proverbs, when it speak to men to guard their ways, it is normally speaking to us to guard our thougth life and to be morally pure.

I'm not referring to covering people's sins who haven't confessed them and made it right with God; but we should not be those who spread people's wrongs all over the place. Be loyal to one another's weakness: Love covers and protects.

    Now I'm not talking about being naive. I'm not talking about hiding some great evil in a corner, but when sin has been exposed and has been dealt with then we cover. Love covers.

    LET US BE THOSE WHO ARE CLOSE-MOUTHED.

    • IT IS DISTORTED or it is part of the facts and what it does is to influence us to form a worng opinion of another person, another work or denomination or situation, and when that happens, we close our spirit off to them and at that meoment we enter into grave sin.
  4. WE HAVE IGNORANCE OF HOW EVIL REPORTS ARE GIVEN.

    They are given by words but some of the most devastating evil reports which I've received or given or have seen given have been in non-verbal communication.
