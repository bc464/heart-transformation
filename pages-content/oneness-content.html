ONENESS OF SPIRIT (Joy Dawson-"Intimate Friendship with God")

  1. God the father, Christ the Son, and the Holy Spirit are equal in authority but different in function.
  2. They complete each other in ministry function - never compete. Often we're unaware where one starts and the other ends; there is a total blending of the Three.
  3. They are totally dependent on each other, based on the humility that knows they desperately need each other.
  4. They have absolute truth in their relationships, therefore, absolute trust.
  5. They glorify one another and serve one another
  6. They have singleness of purpose.
  7. They have absolute holiness in all their relationships and therefore they experience the ultimate in enjoyment of one another.
  8. They are an invincible team, who have an eternal indestructible Kingdom - and who achieve the ultimate in effectiveness.

INVENTORY

  1. What are you trusting Jesus Christ to do for you as you do this course?

  3. Do you like yourself?
  4. What would you most like to change about yourself?
  5. As each fault or weakness in your marraige partner or the people at work or your boss occurs this week, be aware of your reaction to it.
  6. Accept each other as Christ has accepted you.
  7. Focus in on the good qualities of your marraige partner and co-workers and boss and make a list of at least 7 qualities then verbalize your praise to the person and to the Lord.
  8. What would my partner say is my most desirable trait? Ask!
  9. Does my spouse consider me a fun person?
  10. Have I ever felt that the two of us are drifting apart? How?
  11. Do we co-operate in these areas: the raising of our children, the spending of money, decision-making, the use of our time?
