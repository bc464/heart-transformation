Spiritual Warfare

Outcome

  • Baptism of the Holy Spirit and Believers baptism - for us to understand it and how it's necessary for effective spiritual warfare.
  • To understand spiritual conflict and the battle in the spiritual realm and whom Satan is.
  • That our battle is not against flesh and blood but against the evil spirits.
  • To understand and know how to claim the power of Christ.
  • To know our spiritual weapons.
  • To understand that we as humans are Body, Soul and Spirit and how these relate.
  • To know the armour of God as in Ephesians 6:11 and it's meaning.
  • To understand what the Word of God (the NT) says about deliverance. Do Christians need deliverance? Yes.
  • To learn about the gifts of the Holy Spirit.
  • To understand sins and curses and how to deal with it and also generational sins.
  • To learn about the doors where demonic bondage can enter one's life, and to close them.
  • How to keep, and walk in your healing and deliverance.
