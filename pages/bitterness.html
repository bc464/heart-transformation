Dealing with Bitterness

Outcome

  • To understand it and to discern the root of it.
  • To know the effecct of it in one's life and that it will bind one and affect my relationship with God.
  • To learn what the fruit of bitterness is. (There's a long list).
  • To know the stages it can go into:
  • Anger
  • Self-pity
  • Resentment
  • Bitterness
  • Hatred
  • Rebellion
  • Murder
  • How do I know that I have bitterness in my heart? And how to have victory in it through forgiveness.
  • To learn NINE POINTS of self-destruction.
