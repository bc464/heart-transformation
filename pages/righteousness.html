Righteousness versus Self Righteousness

Outcome

  • Self Righteousness- To see one's own bad attitude and heart. To ask God to come and deal with me first.
  • Self Righteousness-To see the pride on one's own heart, which does not allow one to see one's own faults and which prevents one from forgiving.
  • Righteousness-To have integrity, purity of heart, correctness in thinking, feeling and acting. We can only have righteousness in Jesus who is our righteousness, that receive by faith in Him
