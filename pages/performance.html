Performance Orientation

Outcome

  • To ask oneself: "Why am I doing what I am doing?" and "What is the motive of my heart?"
  • To know where this comes from - like: "I will show them" etc.
  • To understand that it is the false motives of our hearts which impels us and that we need to die to the wrong hidden intents in the heart.
  • To learn the character traits of such a person, and how to get the victory.
