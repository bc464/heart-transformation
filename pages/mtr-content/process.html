PROCESS OF TRAINING FOR MINISTRY TEAMS & MONITORS FOR TOUCHING HEARTS MINISTRIES.

TOUCHING HEARTS COURSE: 11 DAY or 18 WEEKS

8-DAY MINISTRY TRAINING

MEMBER OF MINISTRY TEAM OR INTERCESSOR

MINISTRY TEAM LEADER

MONITORING

Complete the Touching Hearts Course and then if you feel called of God to become part of the MInistry Team or an Intercessor, or Monitor, complete the 9-Day Ministry Training. Thereafter, as the next phase of the Training, become part of a Ministry Team when the course is being run again. This provides the practical hands-on training as a Ministry Team member or Intercessor, as you get furhter trained by your Monitor or (Caroline if she is running the course) or a Team-leader. A team leader is one who has more experience and has been released by the Monitor to lead a team. If God were calling one to be a monitor, then, with your local church's blessing, you would then be interviewed by Touching Hearts Ministries before being released into monitoring.
