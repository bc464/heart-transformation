THE SPIRIT OF JEZEBEL

  1. GENERAL OVERVIEW
    1. The Spirit of Jezebel is more involved than the notion of a singular spirit that is easily identified. It is in fact a principality that functions in the area of repentance (sacrifice yourself, repent and then accept righteousness). Jezebel will try everything to prevent you from repenting and receiving righteousness.
    2. It becomes a dog chasing it's tail and the victim is caught between the pit of self-righteousness and the pit of self-condemnation
    3. Jezebel is not only active in an individual, but can be active in a family, in a community, and also in the Church. Jezebel is the daughter of Babylon. In essence anyone who is seduced by Paganism will open the door for the Spirit of Jezebel. (in the seven churches in Revelation we see the seven phases the Church went through. See teaching on Roman Catholosism.)
    4. Immediate discernment of Jezebel is often difficult as it always hides behind a seductive veil of self-righteousness (perfection).
    5. The discernment of a Jezebel spirit will usually come from the evidence of a wake of destruction around that individual. This is most obvious in how they destroy life in those around them, especially their own families.
    6. Another trait is how the initial veil of being very righteous is manipulated to justify perversion of truth. Any opposition to a Jezebel spirit, results in being rejected from that circle.
    7. Jezebel is the DEMON OF SHAME(ROOT). Shame can only exist where there is no repentance of sin. Rom 10:10
    8. The 4 basic needs of the Spirit of Man are a hunger for FELLOWSHIP, INTIMACY, AUTHORITY, and a sense of BELONGING. All spiritual bondage will erodes the victim's capacity to satisy the craving for intimacy. (Intimacy is a want for unconditional love.)
    9. Intimacy = Jezebel tries to confuse the circles. The last circle is covenant ground. There are only 2 covenants = our covenant with Jesus and the marriage covenant.

      NB: Love the sinner, not the son.

      Sexual curses work hand in hand with Jezebel. Cannot give and receive unconditional love. They are unable to receive forgiveness of sin. Shame comes in. A person cannot receive forgiveness because of shame.

      A person who is living under a Jezebel spirit builds walls around himself and Jesus cannot get in there:

      Covenants ground begins when we are placed on the Rock when we accept Jesus in our hearts.

    10. The open door for the spirit is when the victim believes the lie that they are not worthy. In plain language, the first lie of Jezebel is that "You will never make it". Strangely enough the last lie of Jezebel is that "You are the only one who has made it."
    11. Like all demonic influence it is fear driven (begins with Paganism, God is a God of wrath, you have to do something-perform). The mind of the victim spends many hours being obsessed with the problem of being worthy before God. Jesus said, "Fear and axiety and not of Me". (Fear towards person.)
    12. Jezebel always uses the tongue (spirit of seduction, perversion and rejection) to do its work. The soothing tongue to seduce (frog in cold water), the lying tongue to pervert (half truth), and the scatching tongue to reject(spirit of rejection)

      Pilatus: SEDUCTION (Are You the Son of God? Yes) PERVERSION - Half truth (Will you rebuild temple in 3 days? - No answer - will twist no matter what the answer). REJECTION: Crucify Him.

      Pilatus said I can't find anything wrong, crowd said crucify Him.

      Weapon - silence

    13. The Jezebel spirit is a continual and elabirate dance of the above three SEDUCTION (to compromise), PERVERSION (a lack of knowledge) and then REJECTION.

    • Destroy: the leaders especially the Prophets. (The daughter of Jezebel is Athaliah) (No more leaders - a blind church, no discernment, no direction).
    • Destroy: covenant by emasculating the High Priest.(Who Jesus really is), tries to destory covenant with Jesus. Jezebel is a destroyer of covenant ground.
    • Destroy: the Lordship of Jesus. (avoiid understanding of who Jesus is) by seduction into compromise with Paganism.
    • Destroy: intimacy, by substituting true worship with hollow worship. To hold the multitudes captive, powerless and enslaved. (Fear of rejection. Rejection always goes with Jezebel. Rejection is a mighty weapong. We cannot take authority if we have not overcome rejection yet. We cannot become a leader if you have the fear of man (the root = fear of rejection)).

THE ROAD TO IDOLATRY

Matt 21:44 tells us about the stumbling stone of truth that will break men. Jesus Christ who offers salvation by faith and not by works, and thus asks that human pride be humbled(1 Pet 2:6-8).

Jezebel capitalizes on those who stumble.

In fact the spirit goes further, it goes out if its way to deceive many into stumbling on the rock of truth.

"For I bear witness that they have zeal for God, but not according to knowledge. For they being ignorant of God's righteousness, and seeking to establish theit own righteousness, has not submitted to the righteousness of God." For Christ is the end of the law for righteousness to everyone who believes."(Romans 10:2-5).

It was this stumbling block that Christ taught about in his parable about the labourers in the vineyard. Paul in his letter to the Romans pleads to their congregation that they pray for the Jews, because this was a major stumbling block for them. In Paul's letter to the Hebrew he devotes much energy to explaining the old and new Covenants. He also explains in detail the role of the high priest.
Religion and ritual being with it a lie. It is the lie of Jezebel. It is the lie that salvation is earned by religiosity and good works. The word says this about salvation: (Rom 10:9-10)
"...that you confess with your mouth the Lord Jesus and believe in your heart that God has raised Him from the dead, you will be saved. For with the heart one believes unto righteousness (accept it as a gift), and with the mouth confession is made unto salvation."

In order for Satan to place this stumbling nlock before someone, he has to first convince you that you cannot go before the throne of God. This is true if you have not yet done what is required of you: namely that you accept in your heart and confess with your mouth that Jesus Christ is Lord of your life.

Take notice Jezebel's tactice: first tell a half-truth - that God is a Holy God and cannot associate with sin, but it is not true that we are saved by our works & make ourselves righteous before Him.

Once the half-truth has been accepted, then the laborious task of constructing a veil of self-righteousness begins.
This elaborate veil will exude much good intention. Good works, voluntarily going without etc. The only problem is that it persues the Law. A task that is impossible to accomplish.

They stumble on the Rock, they stumble on Jesus, and they fall into Jezebel's embrace.

But surely on the Rock, they stumble on Jesus, and they fall into Jezebel's embrace.

But surely we cannot be condemned for good works? This is true! So where is the problem? Once again Jezebel uses the tactic of a half-truth: It is true that we are expected to do good works, that good works are pleasing to God, but they are not what makes you righteous before God.

"...For with the heart one believes unto righteousness..." (You need humility to repent of your sins)

Jezebel has convinced many that by some reverent ritual, they can make themselves worthy before God. This is an impossible task to accomplish, and God knew that. We just have to humble ourselves enough to accept that He sent His only Son to open the way.

The end result is a veil of self-righteousness. Religion exemplifies this process. In Rev 2:23 Jesus is described as "I am He Who searches the minds and hearts."

In other words the Jezebel veil of perfection will not stand up against His piercing "eyes of fire".

A fool will judge a man by his possessions,
Men can only judge men by their deeds,
But the apolocalyptic Jesus Christ judges men by the motives of their hearts.

Shame blocks many from openly accepting the gift of repentance.
They cannot stand before the mirror of their souls (The brazen laver in Moses' Tabernacle). (You have to look in the mirror and acknowledge who you are.) They build up their veil of lies, to deceive men, to deceive themselves, and perhaps eventually they might even believe they can deceive Jesus.

They fail to accept Jesus' unconditional love, and cannot give or receive inconditional love. They start to worship what they trust in, the false image of their own self-righteousness. Rev. 2:18 onwards.

In the Church the same applies. If a chruch becomes side tracked by ritual and deeper things, believing thay have made themselves more worthy before God, it has achieved nothing more than engaged in the idolatrous worship of its own self-righteous image.

When a church becomes preoccupied with its ritual and traditions. When they begin to preach that this ritual and tradition is a prerequisite for the salvation of its people, then it has fallen into the clutches of Jezebel. Jezebel's final lie will always be the same.

ALL ARE LOST EXCEPT FOR YOU, FOR IT WAS ONLY YOU THAT HAD THE ZEAL TO PERFECT YOURSELF!!
