Facets of Touching Hearts Ministries

EVANGELISM

We are living in such critical times, "the harvest is ripe but the labourers are few", so let's pray for a passion for lost souls. "Making the very most of the time [buying up each opportunity] becuase the days are evil."(Eph 5:16). For monitors and team members to be open and led by the Holy Spirit to use the opportunities that He gives us to bring the lost to Christ.

OVERSEAS MINISTRY
