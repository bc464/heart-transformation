WHY SOME PEOPLE ARE NOT DELIVERED

  • The person does not really know Jesus
  • There is no faith that God is able or willing to heal and deliver.
  • There is no belief that Jesus wants to heal the person.
  • Life is out of balance - in disorder or spiritual chaos.
  • Bitterness and unforgiveness.
  • Unresolved pain or guilt.
  • Praying for the wrong thing.
  • The person believes that "Christians can't have demons" or is possibly under a spiritual covering that holds such a view.
  • There is unconfessed sin which the person is wanting to keep hidden.
  • Ungodly soul-ties and still in place.
  • There is occult powers in place whose control has not been broken either in the person or in those with whom there is a close linking.
  • There is little or no annointing in the place of ministry - due to uncleansed ground, building or organisations.
  • Curses or sickness or death are still in place.
  • Generational sin and / or demonisation has not been dealt with.
  • There is an inner brokenness through trauma and deeper healing is needed.
  • The person is living in denial of the real problem.
  • The person has made inner vows which need to be renounced.
  • The person is in disobedience over something God has already told them to do (which they are not agreeing to.)
  • The ministry team need to seek the Lord for specific revelation about hidden demonic controls affecting the individually concerned.
  • The person doesn't really want to be healed.
  • Person is not under Godly authority.
  • Lack of discipling in the local church.
  • Legalism
  • Blocked memories that need to be released.
  • Passivity
  • The ministry team are not in a right relationship with each other and are blocking the annointing.
  • God's timing.
  • The person is suffering from a sickness that is unto death and needs to be prepared to meet the Lord.
