SUGGESTED GUIDELINES FOR MEMBERS OF THE MINISTRY TEAM OF A TOUCHING HEARTS MINISTRIES

  1. Isa 61. We are instruments in hands of the Holy Spirit. For us to get out of the way, so that the Lord can do His work, we are there to serve others in the Name of Jesus Christ our Lord, what a privilege! To pray for cleansing before any meetings, with the Blood of Jesus.
  2. Intercession. 30 to 15 minutes before the meeting to prepare the ground. Vitally important! Remember to ask the Father where He would have you sit, to be sensitive to Holy Spirit to intercede during the meeting. ("om op jou pos te wees.). During the week to be sensitive to pray for needs in the group, if the Lord gives you a Word for someone to phone and encourage them.
  3. For monitors to remind the group that it is only ministry team that would be ministring to the group, as we want folk to relax and receive to the full for themselves.
  4. After the prayer at end of video, for the monitor to lead the time of ministry, to be waiting on the Lord, being sensitive to what the Holy Spirit is doing, to operate in the gifts of the Holy Spirit with the team.
  5. When praying for people, the ministry team should be aware of the following:
    • Don't pray loudly, just keep it gentle
    • Not to comfort them (Holy SPirit is the Counselor) don't rub the person on back, etc.
    • Not to counsel them (Holy Spirit is the Counselor) not discussing things with them (give it all to God)
    • Not to push them over! (God's power will do it).
    • At the end of ministering to someone, ask the person if there is still something specif they are trusting God for (briefly). If they are deeply engaged with the Lord not to interrupt them.
    • Just gently pray for the Holy Spirit to fill, fall upon, blow upon or be poured out on the person, to wait on Jesus praying quietly in tonques, and minister into the revelation that He gives (Word of knowledge, Discernment, Vision, Prophetic word or Scripture) if the Father does not give any revelation, to encourage the person to give to Jesus whatever He may lift up in their hearts, and to pray into that. Not to pray intellectual or soulish prayers, Whatever you do, if He does not give revelation, then just continue to pray quietly in tongues and release the anointing, to bless what God is blessing and doing. Jesus will show you through the type of anointing as to what He is doing, healing, deliverance, etc.
    • To be praying in teams of 2 or 3 people, one of the team being ready to catch the person if they were to fall - be alert and ready! See that they are covered acceptably. Ensure they will be safe if they were to fall.
    • Pray for them for a while, after they respond to the Holy Spirit in some way, there can be falling, tears, laughter, shaking, etc. or there may be no visible Holy Spirit manifestation, to move on when Jesus tells you.
    • If a fair amount of time goes by and nothing seems to happen, suggest that they give to Jesus whatever He may lift in their hearts, and to stay in the attitude of receiving and soak for a while. Sometimes God may show you what He is doing other times He won't.
    • If people around you, are irreverently watching and laughing, or being noisy, ask them if they wouldn't mind either to be receiving for themselves, or to talk quietly, so as not to distract others who are engaged with the Father. Help maintain a quiet worshipful atmosphere in which the Holy Spirit can strongly minister to each person. Even of some of the manifestations are noisy and dramatic, don't you introduce anything of the flesh with the sacred things that are taking place.
    • Don't delay praying with people, or stand around talking to each other, each time you finish with one person, ask Jesus who He wants you to pray for - let's be efficient, attentive and respectful, ministering to all who God brings our way with His love and gentleness, that Jesus may be glorified.
    • If you are unsure about their salvation, ask if they are saved.
    • If a large group is waiting for ministry and there are few team members try not to spend TOO long with a person praying with the.
    • When people are on the carpet receiving, please don't move them or disturb them or allow their friends or family to disturb them in any way. Encourage them not to be in a hurry to get off the carpet and to work together with the Holy Spirit whatever the Holy Spirit lifts up in the heart to go with that. To soak like a sponge and only to get off the floor when the Father is finished. Often folk get off the carpet too guickly. Don't disturb the person if out on the carpet, be led of God when to share revelation.
    • Ladies should be appropriately dressed.
    • If they battle to receive - to have attitude of "I want to yield to you Jesus" - like woman who pressed through crowd - to relax, not performance, and receive by faith. The capcity to receive will vary from person to person. Things can block one to receive: want to be in control of life; unconfessed sin; grudges; anger in heart toward God; judged work of Holy Spirit; charismatics; is Jesus really Lord?; etc. (would have to be led by Jesus to discern). They would need to repent.
    • Respect their dignity; they are not an object of ministry. "Father what is your heart for this person?" Ask Him to give you His heart of love and compassion. It is a privilege to minister to His body, and beloved Bride!
    • As Christ leads to lay hands on, in a way that does not sistract or embarrass the person, on the head or shoulder if opposite sex to get those on team of the same sex to lay hands on, or get one being prayed for to lay hands on themselves and to lay your hand on top of their hand.
    • Encourage those that do not come forward for ministry at the end of the meeting, to be quietly receiving for themselves, and drinking in the Holy Spirit and letting Him deal with whatever He brings to the surface. Not to be in a hurry to go home! Only to go when Jesus says they are done!
    • Start the meeting promptly! If you don't, people become lax about being on time! Also encourage people to be punctual!
    • Ministry requirements to be remembered:
      1. Submit your revelation to the team leader, not to pray what you have received. The team leader will discern when and how it is to be ministered.
      2. Remember we cannot control someone with our revelation, it is to be submitted to the person being prayed for : eg, if there is fear to sandwich a negative between two positives. eg. "His perfect love comes to drive out fear. If there is fear and that witnesses with you to give it to Jesus. To lay it down at the cross of Jesus, to repent and receive His forgiveness, to receive His love into the place of fear, because He loves you, you can trust Him fully." Remember to turn a negative into a positive, to minister life into it, ask Jesus for the wisdom every time. Not to say, "thus says the Lord".
      3. It is important to work with the person's will, where they can engage with the ministry, not to pray at them. Get the person to release things to God, not to do it for them.
      4. When submitting a revelation to the team leader, to do it in such a way that the person being ministered to does not hear it, or become distracted, draw the team leader to side and whisper in their ear. If the person were on the carpet, not to talk over the person, but come to the side to share revelation with leader.
      5. If it does not witness with the person, not to force it on them, but encourage them to give it to the Lord Jesus and He will confirm it, if it is of Him. We can all miss it, to keep an attitude of submission.
      6. Please not to wave the dream books and notes of "word of knowledge" around, have it at the side of room to use as a reference, remember it is not to be used as a doctrine, pray about revelation and to have it as a confirmation, it is just a guideline.
      7. Don't distrub the person if he/she is out on the carpet; be led of God when to share revelation.
      8. If the person starts to share his/her heart, encourage him/her to give it to the Lord Jesus, not to share it with you, and then to pray about it rather than talk about it.
      9. If the person does not get breakthrough, the team leader should share it with the monitor, to make an appointment at the TH Healing Centre.
      10. The team leader should remember to ask team members if they have received anything from the Lord Jesus.
      11. Remember the covering prayer with the blood of Jesus at the beginning of the meeting and to seal off at the end of the meeting, coming against backlash and pray that the bllod of Jesus would cover any open doors that there may be.
