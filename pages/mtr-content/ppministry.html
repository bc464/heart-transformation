PROPHETIC PRAYER MINISTRY

Prophetic Prayer Ministry is a specific calling requiring a unique gifting and not all people called to become monitors are called to be involved in prophetic prayer ministry. Those that are released to lead a team will be operating in the context of a mini-healing centre, in ministering in more depth to those that have not received breakthrough within the meetings.

Prohetic prayer ministry is linked to prophetic counseling. However we don't call it counseling. The training for prophetic prayer mininstry takes a lot longer +- 1 year( 1 to 4 years). It is not overnight training!

The best form of training is coming alongside and learn from either Caroline or a monitor that has been released to lead a prophetic prayer team, these things are caught than taught. It is a more in-depth and specialized ministry. I believe the Touching Hearts Healing Centres will begin operating when ever the ministry is operating and will develop very naturally. It can have as many teams operating as the Lord leads.

When a monitor or team member is ready to be released then they can lead the second team, which can be developed into a third team etc., so that a healing centre can develop in a very natural way. The 2nd and 3rd teams will operate in the healing centre where the lead monitor can be available to ooversee the ministry that takes place and be available to support and help if necessary. Some monitors may be called to be part of a prophetic prayer ministry team, but may not necessaruly be called to lead such a team.

As a ministry we do not give our blessing for ministry team members to be counseling outside the meetings. If they see that folk are needing counseling or more in depth ministry, they are to encourage them to make appointments with the lead monitor (or their pastor or local churches) and come along with that person and be part of the team ministering to such a person, because they are not equipping to operate in PPM. We as a ministry cannot sanction it or take responsibility for it. PPM is also somehting that doesn't take place within the TH meetings. It is a different ministry! Not every monitor and ministry team member is called to PPM. No amount of training may necessarily prepare one for it, because it simply is not one's calling.

It does not just depend on gifting, but also the ability to work with people, because generally it is caught not taught. That is why I don't spend a lot of time teaching on it as the MTR and owuld like to encourage those that want to be involved with it to come to be trained by Caroline or the lead monitor, in their area, that has been released for PPM.
