Idolatry

Outcome

  • To learn what God says: "You shall have no other gods before Me or besides Me."
  • To be able to discer all the idols in one's life.
  • To learn the difference between idols and icons and how they relate.
  • To learn that our very first calling in life is to be a worshiper of the living God and to love Him 1st above all else.
